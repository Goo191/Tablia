About Us

More about Tabliah story

Our journey began years ago, when our family would gather around Tabliah, sharing special moments over dishes that carried the rich history and flavors of Egypt. Those moments inspired us to share this heritage with others. Our idea was simple: to offer an authentic experience that blends traditional Egyptian flavors with the warmth and hospitality our country is known for. From the very beginning, we committed to using fresh, local ingredients to make each dish a unique experience. Whether it's the famous falafel and fava beans, the flavorful koshary, or even the sweet treats like basbousa and kunafa, we serve each plate with the same love and passion that our mothers and grandmothers did. Our goal is to take guests on a journey to the heart of Egypt with every meal, making them feel as though they are in a true Egyptian home, filled with warmth and generosity.We are proud to be part of this rich heritage and look forward to sharing the flavors of Egypt with everyone who steps into our restaurant.
